The Rolex Difference

Every Rolex is built to last generations. Here's what sets us apart.

🛠

In-House Movement

Every component — from the hairspring to the escapement — is designed and manufactured in our own facilities.

📏

Superlative Chronometer

Each watch is certified to an accuracy of −2/+2 seconds per day — twice the precision of a standard chronometer.

🔬

904L Oystersteel

We use 904L steel — a superalloy that resists corrosion and polishes to an unmatched luster found only on Rolex watches.

👁

Cyclops Lens

The signature magnifying lens over the date window — virtually scratchproof and a hallmark of Rolex craftsmanship since 1953.

🌊

Waterproof to Extremes

From the Submariner's 300m depth rating to the Deepsea's 3,900m — every Oyster case is individually pressure-tested.

💰

Holds Its Value

Rolex watches consistently appreciate over time. A purchase today is an heirloom — and an investment — for tomorrow.
